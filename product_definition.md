### Product Definition: theademo Calculator

#### Overview
`theademo` is a command-line driven calculator application designed to perform basic and complex arithmetic operations.

#### Features
- **Basic Arithmetic**: Supports Addition (`+`), Subtraction (`-`), Multiplication (`*`), and Division (`/`).
- **Order of Operations**: Follows the **PEMDAS** rule:
  - **P**: Parentheses
  - **E**: Exponents (optional but recommended for completeness)
  - **M**: Multiplication
  - **D**: Division
  - **A**: Addition
  - **S**: Subtraction
- **Parentheses Support**: Allows users to group operations using `(` and `)` to override standard precedence.
- **CLI Interface**: Interactive command-line interface for inputting expressions and receiving results.
